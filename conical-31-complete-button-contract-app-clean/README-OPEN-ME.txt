This is the complete modular editor app.

Open index.html in your browser.

Required files in this folder:
- index.html  (small shell file, about 41 KB)
- styles.css  (all app styles)
- app.js      (main app logic, about 1.5 MB)
- component-contracts-sidecar.json

Do not delete app.js or styles.css. The small index.html is normal because the app is now modular.
